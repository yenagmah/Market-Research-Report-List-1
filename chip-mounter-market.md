<p><strong>The Chip Mounter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount Technology (SMT) Equipment</li><li>Through hole technology (THT) Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip mounter markets encompass two primary types: Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology (THT). SMT involves mounting electronic components directly onto the surface of printed circuit boards, utilizing automated equipment for high precision and efficiency, ideal for compact designs. In contrast, THT focuses on inserting leads through holes in the PCB, often providing greater mechanical strength. Both methods serve distinct applications, and the choice between them depends on factors like production volume, component type, and assembly cost considerations.</p></p>
