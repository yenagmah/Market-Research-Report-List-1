<p><p>The ultrasonic energy devices market includes various types such as Harmonic, Thunderbeat, and Sonicision systems. Harmonic type devices use high-frequency vibrations for cutting and coagulating tissue, ensuring minimal thermal damage. Thunderbeat combines ultrasonic and electric energy, enhancing effectiveness in hemostasis and tissue dissection. Sonicision, on the other hand, utilizes a unique design for efficient tissue handling while delivering consistent energy for cutting. Each type plays a crucial role in modern surgical procedures, improving precision and patient outcomes.</p></p>
