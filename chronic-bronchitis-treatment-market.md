<p><p>The chronic bronchitis treatment market encompasses several key types of medications. Antibiotics address bacterial infections that may exacerbate the condition. Anti-inflammatory drugs, including corticosteroids, reduce airway inflammation, easing symptoms. Bronchodilators help open the airways, improving airflow and reducing breathlessness. Mucolytics thin mucus, facilitating easier clearance from the airways. Together, these treatments aim to alleviate symptoms, enhance quality of life, and manage the chronic nature of bronchitis effectively. Each type plays a vital role in comprehensive patient care.</p></p>
