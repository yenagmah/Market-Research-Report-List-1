<p><ul><li>Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS)</li><li>Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation (EMS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Electro-Simulators market includes Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) and Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation (EMS) devices. TENS units are primarily used for pain relief by sending electrical impulses through the skin to interfere with pain signals. In contrast, EMS devices stimulate muscles directly, promoting recovery and enhancing strength through controlled electrical currents. Both types of simulators are widely adopted in physical therapy, rehabilitation, and pain management, contributing to their growing market presence as non-invasive treatment options.</p></p>
