<p><p>Disaster recovery software is categorized into two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises disaster recovery involves local servers and infrastructure, providing businesses with direct control and customized setups, but requiring significant investment in hardware and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based disaster recovery leverages remote servers and services, offering scalability, cost-efficiency, and ease of management, as data is automatically backed up and can be accessed from anywhere. Each type serves distinct organizational needs and resource availability.</p></p>
