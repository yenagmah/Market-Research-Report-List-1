<p><p>The Mobile Sterilization Solutions market comprises two primary types: Closed Loop and Open Loop systems. Closed Loop sterilization involves a self-contained process where waste is managed internally, ensuring minimal environmental impact and efficient resource use. In contrast, Open Loop systems allow for external waste treatment and typically involve transporting waste to centralized facilities. Both types cater to varying needs of healthcare and food industries, ensuring effective sterilization while addressing regulatory and sustainability concerns.</p></p>
