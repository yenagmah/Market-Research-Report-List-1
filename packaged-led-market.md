<p><p>The packaged LED market includes three main types: visible LEDs, infrared (IR) LEDs, and ultraviolet (UV) LEDs. Visible LEDs emit light within the visible spectrum, commonly used in displays and lighting applications. IR LEDs are used in remote sensors, communication, and thermal imaging, as they emit wavelengths that are invisible to the human eye. UV LEDs produce ultraviolet light, primarily employed in sterilization, curing processes, and advanced lighting applications. Each type serves distinct functionalities across various industries, driving innovation and growth.</p></p>
