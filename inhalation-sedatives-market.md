<p><ul><li>Sevoflurane</li><li>Desflurane</li><li>Isoflurane</li><li>Nitrous Oxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inhalation sedatives market includes agents like Sevoflurane, Desflurane, Isoflurane, and Nitrous Oxide, each with unique properties for anesthesia. Sevoflurane is commonly favored for its rapid onset and recovery profile, making it suitable for outpatient procedures. Desflurane offers even quicker recovery but can be irritating to airways. Isoflurane is stable and effective for maintenance anesthesia. Nitrous Oxide is often used for sedation and analgesia due to its rapid action and safety profile, making it versatile in various settings.</p></p>
