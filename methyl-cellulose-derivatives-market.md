<p><p>Methyl cellulose derivatives include Hydroxyethyl Methyl Cellulose (HEMC), Hydroxybutyl Methyl Cellulose (HBMC), and Hydroxypropyl Methyl Cellulose (HPMC). HEMC is widely used as a thickening agent in pharmaceuticals, food, and cosmetics due to its excellent water retention properties. HBMC offers enhanced viscosity and is utilized in various applications, including construction and coatings. HPMC is highly valued for its versatility in industries like paint, adhesives, and personal care, thanks to its film-forming and emulsifying characteristics.</p></p>
