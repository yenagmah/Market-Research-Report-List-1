<p><p>The light therapy equipment market includes three main types: Conventional, LED, and Fiberoptic. Conventional type devices use traditional light sources like fluorescent bulbs and are often bulkier. LED type equipment utilizes light-emitting diodes for targeted treatments, offering energy efficiency and portability. Fiberoptic type devices employ fiberoptic cables to deliver light precisely to specific areas, allowing for versatile treatment options and reduced heat emission. Each type caters to different therapeutic needs and preferences, expanding usage across various medical and wellness applications.</p></p>
