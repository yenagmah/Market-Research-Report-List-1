<p><p>The granular applicator market is segmented into two main types: demountable and self-propelled applicators. Demountable applicators are designed to be attached and detached from a towing vehicle, providing flexibility for different tasks and vehicles. In contrast, self-propelled applicators are autonomous machines equipped with their own propulsion systems, offering increased mobility and efficiency in applying granular materials. Both types cater to varying agricultural needs and operational efficiencies in the application of fertilizers, herbicides, and other granular products.</p></p>
