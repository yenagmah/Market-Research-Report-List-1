<p><p>The Overwrap Films market is primarily divided into two types: coated and uncoated films. Coated overwrap films feature a specialized layer that enhances barrier properties, moisture resistance, and printability, making them suitable for high-quality packaging applications. In contrast, uncoated overwrap films lack this additional layer, offering more basic protection and flexibility, often used for cost-effective packaging solutions.
