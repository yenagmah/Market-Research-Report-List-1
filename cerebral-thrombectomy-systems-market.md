<p><p>The cerebral thrombectomy systems market encompasses various technologies for removing blood clots from cerebral arteries. Ultrasound-assisted thrombectomy systems utilize ultrasound energy to break down clots. Rheolytic thrombectomy systems employ saline jet streams to fragment clots. Rotational thrombectomy systems use a rotating device to capture and remove clots mechanically. Aspiration thrombectomy systems create suction to directly retrieve clots. Other systems may include innovative or hybrid technologies, enhancing the spectrum of options available for treating acute ischemic strokes.</p></p>
