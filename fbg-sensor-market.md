<p><strong>The FBG Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point FPG</li><li>Distributed FPG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber Bragg grating (FBG) sensor market is primarily categorized into point FBG and distributed FBG systems. Point FBG sensors measure specific parameters at discrete locations, offering high sensitivity and accuracy for applications like structural monitoring and temperature measurement. In contrast, distributed FBG sensors utilize a continuous fiber optic cable to provide spatially resolved measurements along its length, enabling monitoring of extensive structures or environments. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as civil engineering, aerospace, and energy.</p></p>
