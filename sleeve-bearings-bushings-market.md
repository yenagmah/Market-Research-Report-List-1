<p><p>Sleeve bearings, also known as bushings, are critical components in machinery, facilitating smooth rotation and reducing friction between moving parts. The market for sleeve bearings is categorized into radial and axial types. Radial sleeve bearings support loads perpendicular to the shaft, commonly used in motors and gearboxes. Axial sleeve bearings, on the other hand, handle loads parallel to the shaft, often found in applications like thrust applications. Both types are essential for enhancing performance and longevity of mechanical systems.</p></p>
