<p><ul><li>SA (Stand Alone)</li><li>NSA (Non-Stand Alone)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G macro site market can be categorized into two main types: Stand-Alone (SA) and Non-Stand-Alone (NSA). SA architecture operates independently with its own core network, enabling full 5G capabilities, enhanced speed, and lower latency, making it ideal for advanced applications. In contrast, NSA leverages existing 4G infrastructure while integrating 5G, allowing for quicker deployment and transitional benefits. Both market types play crucial roles in expanding 5G coverage and enhancing mobile services, catering to diverse consumer and business needs.</p></p>
