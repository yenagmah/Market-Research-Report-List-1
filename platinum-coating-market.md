Solvent-based coatings use a liquid carrier to dissolve the coating materials, offering excellent adhesion and versatility but often emit volatile organic compounds. Other coating methods may include liquid or ceramic coatings, which cater to specific requirements in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
