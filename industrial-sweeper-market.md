<p><p>The industrial sweeper market features two main types: walk-behind sweepers and rider-on sweepers. In contrast, rider-on sweepers are larger, allowing operators to sit while cleaning, which increases efficiency in larger areas such as warehouses and parking lots. Both types cater to distinct operational needs, ensuring effective cleaning solutions across various industrial environments.</p></p>
