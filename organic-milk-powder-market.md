<p><p>The organic milk powder market is segmented into several types: skimmed milk, whole milk, buttermilk, and whey. Skimmed milk powder contains minimal fat, appealing to health-conscious consumers. Whole milk powder retains all natural fats, offering a rich flavor and texture. Buttermilk powder, derived from the butter-making process, is used in baking and cooking for its tangy flavor. Whey powder, a byproduct of cheese production, is high in protein and commonly used in various formulations, including protein supplements and nutritional products.</p></p>
