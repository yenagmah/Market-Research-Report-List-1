<p><p>The disposable surgical gloves market comprises three primary types: natural rubber gloves, vinyl disposable gloves, and nitrile disposable gloves. Natural rubber gloves offer excellent elasticity and tactile sensitivity, making them popular in various medical settings. Vinyl disposable gloves are cost-effective and suitable for low-risk tasks, though they provide less protection than other types. Nitrile disposable gloves are known for their durability and resistance to punctures and chemicals, making them ideal for higher-risk environments, combining safety and comfort for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
