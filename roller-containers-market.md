<p><p>Roller containers are categorized into two main market types: Open Vision and Covered Vision. Open Vision roller containers feature an unobstructed design, allowing easy visibility and access to stored items, making them ideal for situations where quick retrieval is essential. Conversely, Covered Vision roller containers come with a lid or cover, providing protection from external factors and enhancing security. Both types serve various industries, including logistics and retail, by improving efficiency in the transportation and storage of goods.</p></p>
