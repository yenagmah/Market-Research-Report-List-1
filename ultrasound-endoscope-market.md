<p><p>The ultrasound endoscope market features two main types: soft endoscopes and rigid endoscopes. Soft endoscopes are flexible, allowing for easier navigation in complex anatomical structures, making them suitable for procedures such as gastrointestinal examinations. Rigid endoscopes, on the other hand, are inflexible, providing stability and high precision in certain surgical applications. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities, improve patient outcomes, and are essential tools in various medical fields, including gastroenterology and urology, driving advancements in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
