<p><strong>The Chip Multilayer Diplexer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Time Division Duplex"</li><li>"Frequency Division Duplex"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chip Multilayer Diplexer market includes two primary types: Time Division Duplex (TDD) and Frequency Division Duplex (FDD). TDD allows for the transmission and reception of signals over the same frequency channel by dividing time into slots, making it efficient for data burst communications. In contrast, FDD utilizes separate frequency bands for simultaneous transmission and reception, enabling continuous communication and reducing latency. Both technologies cater to different applications in telecommunications, enhancing overall network performance and flexibility.</p></p>
