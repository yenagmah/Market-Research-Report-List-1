<p><p>The Medical Suspended Bed market includes Sand Suspended Beds and Air Pulsating Suspension Beds, both designed to enhance patient comfort and recovery. Sand Suspended Beds utilize a layer of sand to evenly distribute weight, reducing pressure points and promoting better circulation. Conversely, Air Pulsating Suspension Beds use air technology to create a dynamic support system, adjusting continuously to the patient's movements. Both types aim to prevent pressure ulcers, facilitate healing, and ensure optimal comfort during hospital stays or rehabilitation.</p></p>
