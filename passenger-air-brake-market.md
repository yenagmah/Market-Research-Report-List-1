<p><strong>The Passenger Air Brake Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Disc Brake</li><li>Air Drum Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger air brake market comprises two main types: air disc brakes and air drum brakes. Air disc brakes utilize a flat rotor and caliper system, offering efficient heat dissipation and consistent performance, making them popular for modern vehicles. In contrast, air drum brakes consist of a cylindrical drum and shoe mechanism, providing robust braking power and traditionally suited for heavier vehicles. Both systems play crucial roles in ensuring safety and performance in passenger transport, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
