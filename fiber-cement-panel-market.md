<p><p>The fiber cement panel market is categorized into three types based on density: low, medium, and high density. Low-density fiber cement panels are lightweight and suitable for applications requiring minimal structural load, often used in interiors. Medium-density panels offer a balance of weight and durability, making them versatile for both residential and commercial exteriors. High-density fiber cement panels are the heaviest and strongest, providing superior resistance to weather and impact, ideal for demanding environments and structural applications.</p></p>
